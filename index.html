What we do

A single recurring service for the cleanup work property managers shouldn't have to think about.

Litter & debris removal

Daily, weekly, or custom-frequency walks across parking lots, sidewalks, and curb edges to remove litter, cigarette butts, and small debris.

Landscape bed perimeter

Hand-picking litter from mulch beds, planters, and curb gardens where blown debris collects and groundskeepers don't reach.

Incident & storm cleanup

Response cleanup for illegally dumped materials, broken receptacles, and post-storm debris, billed per occurrence with prior approval.

Incident reporting with photo evidence

Dumped materials, vandalism, or out-of-scope conditions get photographed and sent to you before any additional work is billed. No surprises on the invoice.

After-hours service

Cleanup happens before tenants open and after they close, so service never disrupts the property's business day or customer experience.

Fully insured

Commercial General Liability with property owner and management firm named as additional insureds. Certificate available on request.

How we work

From a phone call to a signed agreement in a matter of days.

i.

Site Assessment

We walk your property, assess conditions, and identify the high-accumulation zones and access points that affect service.

ii.

Written Proposal

You receive a proposal with frequency options, monthly pricing, scope of service, and contract terms within five business days.

iii.

Service Begins

Once the agreement is signed, scheduled visits start the following week with full photo documentation from day one.

iv.

Ongoing Reporting

Service summaries land in your inbox on the cadence you choose, so you have documentation for your own reporting up the chain.
